Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 11 September 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the Max Photo Frames app (“the App”), developed by Max8Tech. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how the App handles information.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, share, or otherwise access any of your personal information. The App is designed to function entirely on your device.

Photo Access

The App requires access to your photo library so you can select and edit photos. This access is used solely for that purpose. All processing happens locally on your device. Photos are never uploaded to our servers or accessible to us.

In-App Purchases

The App offers optional in-app purchases for tips. All transactions are processed securely by Apple through the App Store’s official payment system. We do not receive or store any of your payment details, such as credit card numbers.

Usage Data

The App does not include analytics or tracking tools. Preferences such as default frame colour or font are stored locally on your device and are not shared with us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised “Last Updated” date. You are encouraged to review this page periodically.
